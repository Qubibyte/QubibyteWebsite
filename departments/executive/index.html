Executive Department

Leading strategic direction and driving the vision that powers Qubibyte's innovation in quantum computing.

Overview

The Executive Department at Qubibyte serves as the strategic nucleus of our organization, responsible for setting the company's vision, mission, and long-term goals. Our executive leadership team works collaboratively to ensure all departments are aligned with our core objective of advancing quantum computing technology for real-world applications.

Responsibilities

  • Strategic planning and vision setting for long-term growth
  • Oversight and coordination across all organizational departments
  • High-level decision-making on company direction and investments
  • External representation and stakeholder management
  • Establishing corporate culture and values
  • Risk management and governance oversight

Leadership

Our executive team is led by Trent Rosenthal, Chief Executive Officer, who has been at the helm since January 1st, 2025. The executive department works in close collaboration with department heads across Operations, Financial, Technology, Marketing, and Legal to ensure cohesive execution of Qubibyte's mission.

Trent Rosenthal - Chief Executive Officer
Tenure: January 1st, 2025 - Present

Vision

The Executive Department is committed to positioning Qubibyte as a global leader in quantum computing solutions. We envision a future where quantum computing is accessible, practical, and transformative across industries including nuclear fusion, materials science, pharmaceuticals, and cybersecurity. Our leadership ensures that every decision moves us closer to making quantum computing "Everywhere, All at Once."
