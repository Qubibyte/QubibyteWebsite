Legal Department

Ensuring compliance, protecting intellectual property, and maintaining legal integrity across all operations.

Overview

The Legal Department at Qubibyte ensures that all company operations comply with applicable laws and regulations while protecting the company's intellectual property and interests. We provide legal guidance across all departments, manage contracts, handle regulatory matters, and ensure that Qubibyte operates with the highest standards of legal integrity.

Responsibilities

  • Intellectual property protection and patent management
  • Contract negotiation and review
  • Regulatory compliance and risk assessment
  • Corporate governance and legal structure
  • Employment law and HR legal matters
  • Privacy and data protection compliance
  • Litigation management and dispute resolution

Protecting Innovation

In the rapidly evolving field of quantum computing, protecting intellectual property is crucial. Our Legal Department works closely with the Technology Department to identify, protect, and manage patents, trademarks, and trade secrets. We ensure that Qubibyte's innovative quantum computing architectures, programming languages, and software are properly safeguarded while respecting the intellectual property rights of others.

Ensuring Compliance

As Qubibyte operates in a highly regulated technology sector, compliance is paramount. Our Legal Department monitors regulatory developments, ensures adherence to export control regulations (especially relevant for quantum computing technology), data protection laws, and industry-specific requirements. We work proactively to identify and mitigate legal risks, allowing the company to focus on innovation while maintaining legal integrity.

Leadership

The Legal Department is led by Trent Rosenthal, Chief Legal Officer (Acting), who has been serving in this acting capacity since November 23rd, 2025. The legal team ensures compliance, manages intellectual property, and handles contracts, protecting Qubibyte's interests and maintaining legal integrity across all operations.
