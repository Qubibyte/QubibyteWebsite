Marketing Department

Building brand awareness and connecting Qubibyte's quantum computing innovations with the world.

Overview

The Marketing Department at Qubibyte is responsible for building brand awareness, engaging with our community, and driving market growth for our quantum computing solutions. We translate complex quantum computing concepts into compelling narratives that resonate with researchers, developers, businesses, and enthusiasts worldwide.

Responsibilities

  • Brand strategy and positioning
  • Content creation and marketing campaigns
  • Digital marketing and social media management
  • Public relations and media outreach
  • Community engagement and events
  • Market research and customer insights
  • Product marketing and launch strategies

Telling Our Story

Quantum computing is a complex field, and our Marketing Department excels at making it accessible and exciting. Through our blog, resources, social media presence, and community engagement, we share the story of how Qubibyte is making quantum computing "Everywhere, All at Once." We showcase the practical applications and real-world impact of our technology while building an engaged community of quantum computing enthusiasts.

Building the Future

Our marketing efforts focus on educating the market about quantum computing's potential while positioning Qubibyte as an innovative leader in the space. We work closely with the Technology Department to understand new developments and communicate them effectively. Our goal is to build a strong brand that represents innovation, accessibility, and the transformative power of quantum computing.

Leadership

The Marketing Department is led by Trent Rosenthal, Chief Marketing Officer (Acting), who has been serving in this acting capacity since November 23rd, 2025. The marketing team builds brand awareness, engages with customers, and drives market growth, communicating Qubibyte's innovative solutions to the world.
